Graphic Arts Institute is a technology oriented monotechnic institute in Mohammadpur, the capital of Bangladesh. It is administered under the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE). It is the only government printing based monotechnic institute for engineering courses in Bangladesh. It offers diploma in engineering programs in three technologies, they are 1. Printing technology, 2. Graphic design techniques and, 3. Computer Science Technology.

Institute of Design, it offers our four-year technical course called Diploma in Printing Engineering. Governor of East Pakistan Mr. Azam Khan and then Director General of Technical Education Dr. Waqar Ahmed were the main initiators. Dr. RK Mollah was appointed as the Founder Principal (1967-1972) of the Institute of Graphic Art. He was sent to the University of South Dakota in the United States for advanced training in printing technology. Other trainers of the Institute were also trained at the Central Government Printing Press. The institute started with 25 seats.
But currently this institute has more than hundred students in two shifts (morning and evening). There are three departments namely Graphic Design, Printing Technology and Computer Technology. Every year around 400 students get admission in this institute.
